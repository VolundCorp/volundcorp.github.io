Volund

Need help about building your own Discord server? Volund is here to help!

With Volund, you will be able to:
  • Create voice lobbies (Voice channels to create temporary voice channels)
  • Create categories: A private category with its own role, text channels, news channel and voice lobby. Highly customizable.
  • Create welcome message
The most interesting thing about Volund is its mini-game: a mine! With this mine, you will be able to dig and try to harvest ores.
This ore is necessary to improve your server:
  • To unlock a feature (i.e. welcome message)
  • To improve features such as categories (categories limit increase)
Each user has their own ore purse and can decide whether or not to invest (i.e. give money) in your server!

Setup

  • /setup - Creates Volund's forge (where magic happens)
  • /setup-mine - Creates mine (to earn ore)

Usage

  • /invest - To give money to the current server
  • /unlock - To unlock a feature (i.e. welcome message)
  • /actually - To tell interesting facts
  • /info - To get current server's information
  • /about - To get information about Volund
