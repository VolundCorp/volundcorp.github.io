Forge

By using /setup, you'll create Volund's forge.

The forge is the foundation of Volund. Thanks to the it, you will be able to perform several actions linked to the bot:

  • Create a category: These custom sections can contain as many text channels as you want, a news channel and a voice lobby. It is up to you to choose among these different channels to put in your category.
  • Create a voice lobby:
  • Upgrade your own tool:
  • Increase categories limit:
  • Set welcome message: Volund is capable of welcome your newcomers with custom message.
    You'll have to unlock it with /unlock first.
  • Increase mine chance: each time you upgrade your mine, it will gain 5% more chance to find ores!
